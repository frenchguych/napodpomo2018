Welcome, visitor !

It is November again, so it's time for another go at NaPodPoMo.

"But Yannick", I hear you say, "what is NaPodPoMo ?"

I will quote the kind of official website :

NaPodPoMo is a month long event along the same vein as National Novel Writing Month aka NaNoWriMo. The difference? Well, instead of writing a 50,000 word novel, you podcast every day for 30 days from November 1st-30th.

So, let's give that a try ! But be warned, this year, I am not ready : I haven't prepared anything, I barely finished this website on time, and I'm not playing any music !

With that being said, I hope you will enjoy this 2018 NaPodPoMo series !
